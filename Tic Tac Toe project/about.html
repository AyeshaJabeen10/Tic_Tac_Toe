About Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game,
where players take turns marking spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid.
The objective is to place three respective marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.

This web version allows you to play against another player or challenge the computer.
The computer uses the Minimax algorithm to make optimal moves.
